CV-COUNT Walkthrough & Guide (v1.6)
==================================================

1. INSTALLATION
   Run: "02. Setup (Run first).bat"
   - This "Smart Installer" will scan your system hardware.
   - NVIDIA GPU: Offers to install the 2.6GB CUDA package.
   - Apple Silicon (M1/M2/M3): Automatically configures Metal (MPS) support.
   - It also handles all other libraries and the assets folder automatically.

2. STARTING THE APP
   Run: "03. Start CV Count App (Run after 02).bat"

3. NEW USER INTERFACE (UX)
   - Home Screen: Professional Slate (Dark Mode) & Emerald Green.
   - Animations: Look for the pulsing "LAUNCH" button and hover effects.
   - Tooltips: Hover over any setting in the menu to see a description in the bar at the bottom.
   - Close Button: You can now click the standard "X" on the window to exit.

4. DRAWING CONTROLS (Setup Phase)
   - PRESS [N]: To draw a LINE. (Click 2 points)
   - PRESS [Z]: To draw a ZONE (Polygon). (Click multiple points)
   - PRESS [Backspace]: To undo the last point.
   - PRESS [SPACE]: To START COUNTING.
   - Drawing is "Pixel Perfect" -- click exactly where you want on your screen, no matter your video's resolution.

5. ASSETS
   - Place your videos in the "assets/" folder.
   - Counted videos are also saved here by default.

6. HARDWARE
   - NVIDIA GPU support via CUDA.
   - Apple Silicon support via Metal (MPS).
